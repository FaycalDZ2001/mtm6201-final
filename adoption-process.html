Adoption Process

Learn how to give a forever home to your new best friend.

Find Pet

Browse our available pets and choose the companion that fits your family and lifestyle.

Submit Application

Fill out the adoption form so we can learn more about you and your home environment.

Finalize Adoption

Meet your new friend, review the paperwork, and bring your adopted pet home.

Adoption Fees

  • Dogs: $80 adoption fee (includes microchip, vaccinations, spay/neuter).
  • Cats: $60 adoption fee (includes microchip, vaccinations, spay/neuter).
  • Rabbits and small pets: $30 adoption fee.
  • All adoption fees support food, medical care, and shelter.

Requirements

  • Must be 18 years or older.
  • Valid photo ID is required.
  • Proof of address (utility bill or lease).
  • Landlord approval for renters.
  • All family members must agree to adoption.
  • Willingness for home visit or follow-up.
